  # **Pluto**
</div>

What happens when humans try to create robots which are as close as possible to humans? _Pluto_ is a mystery anime about the coexistence of humans and robots in a futuristic world. There is a serial killer on the loose targeting a group of robots who are considered as heroes of the current generation. And it is upto the main character Gesict to solve this mystery who has a post of his own to uncover.

If you are a fan of mystery anime then this one is definitely a must watch as it is from the author Naoki Urasawa who has authored other famous works like _Monster_.
This anime has a stellar dub performance from the voice actors and the animation quality is very good.
