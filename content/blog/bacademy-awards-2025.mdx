# BACademy Awards 2025

## Nomination and Award Process

- **BAC admin team chooses jury** \
  Jury members are club members and prolific anime watchers carefully chosen to cover widest range of 2024 titles

- **Jury chooses categories and nominees** \
  Extensive dialogue between jury members determines this; shortlists are merged to create final consensus lists

- **Voting opened for People’s Choice categories** \
  Jury decides which categories to open for public voting; Google Form shared on BAC groups

- **Public voting period ends; jury discusses Jury Choice candidates** \
  These take place simultaneously to avoid the former biasing the latter

- **Jury Choice decided; final awards ceremony** \
  Internal voting and debate used to arrive at consensus

## Nomination Rules and Considerations

- **Anime Series** \
  Must have **started airing in 2024**

- **Anime Films** \
  Must have **screened in India in 2024**. Films are not eligible for Audiovisual prize categories, in interest of fairness.

- **Manga** \
  Must have **5 volumes or less in English** by end of 2024. Must be **no older than 2019**. Must not be a reprint.

- **Characters** \
  Must have **debuted** or **shown significant character evolution** in 2024. Need not be in an eligible anime.

- **Country of Origin** \
  Anime (series and films) must be Japanese productions animated by Japanese studios; source material need not be Japanese. Manga must be published in a Japanese manga magazine.
